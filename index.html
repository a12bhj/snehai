Our Royal Heritage

Rajwada Hotel stands as a tribute to India's rich royal heritage, blending timeless traditions with contemporary luxury. Established in 1995, our hotel has been serving distinguished guests with royal hospitality for over two decades.

The architecture and interiors are inspired by the grand palaces of Rajasthan, featuring intricate carvings, majestic domes, and ornate decor that transport you to an era of kings and queens.

Our team is trained in the finest traditions of Indian hospitality, ensuring every guest feels like royalty from the moment they arrive.
